5G Advanced and 6G

5G Advanced is an evolution of the existing 5G network technology. It aims to enhance the capabilities of 5G with faster speeds, lower latency, and improved connectivity, paving the way for more advanced applications like augmented reality (AR) and virtual reality (VR).

6G, on the other hand, is the next generation of wireless technology. While it's still in the early stages of development, it is expected to provide even faster speeds, near-instantaneous communication, and support for groundbreaking technologies like holographic communication and advanced AI applications.
